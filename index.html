What We Do

SewerCam is independent. We’re not plumbers or sewer contractors, so you can be sure that our inspection camera technicians will only report what’s there. Our sewer inspection camera technicians are clean, courteous, and experienced.

What We Charge

We charge $215 for a base inspection, with a $30 access fee if no sewer cleanout is available. If you’re a plumber or sewer contractor that only needs a locate, we charge $175. If you’ve had us inspect your side sewer already, but need us to inspect again, we offer a discounted rate of $150.

Where We Work

SewerCam exclusively serves the South King County area, to include Federal Way, Auburn, Kent, Des Moines, SeaTac, and Burien.
